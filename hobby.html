My favourite painter is Wlop from China

"Ghost Blade" is an original picture comic book from Wlop, which is now serialized in Bilibili Comics. The cartoon takes Norah Mainland, where human beings and the Yu tribe live together, as the story stage, and tells about the feud and disputes between various forces in Norah Mainland.

Once upon a time, on the land of Norah, in honor of the goddess Norah, pious people built a magnificent tower.
Norah goddess for people's piety moved, on the top of the tower opened a door to the other world - the eye of heaven.
Soon the world was visited by the Yu, a race from another world. The Yu people quickly integrated into human society.
They brought with them many precious materials and taught people how to make iron and steel, develop agriculture, and even teach people how to speak a common language. It is because of these friends from heaven that human society develops rapidly.
Therefore, people give these friendly friends a beautiful name -- "Angel".
